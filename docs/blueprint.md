# **App Name**: Gattwala Academy

## Core Features:

- School Information: Display school information such as address, contact details, and a brief introduction to the school's mission and values.
- Admissions Portal: Allow prospective students and parents to access and manage their admissions process, submit all requested documentation, and check for status updates.
- News & Events: Showcase the latest news, events, and announcements happening in the school. Use AI to find important implicit details in the raw input of these details, such as important names, topics, or dates; the AI tool can then highlight this data.
- Image Gallery: A collection of professional, high-quality photos of the school campus.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#64B5F6) to evoke trust and tranquility. It suggests reliability and calmness, essential for an educational institution.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E3F2FD), almost white, creating a clean and open feel that encourages focus and readability.
- Accent color: Yellow-Orange (#FFAB40), approximately 30 degrees to the left of the primary hue, bringing in a contrasting yet complementary element that signals warmth and approachability, while still ensuring important details stand out.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif with high contrast and an elegant feel. Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif which pairs nicely with Playfair. Both for headlines and body.
- Use clean and modern icons to represent different sections and features of the school website.
- Ensure a clear, intuitive layout with easy navigation for parents, students, and staff.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience and guide interaction without being distracting.
